# Instructions for Setting Up "Send Mail As" in Gmail

## Email Template to Send to BAs

**Subject:** Action Required: Set Up "Send Mail As" for Property Review System

---

Hi [BA Name],

To enable the Property Review System to send emails on your behalf, please follow these steps to set up "Send mail as" in your Gmail account:

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Open Gmail Settings**
   - Click the gear icon (⚙️) in the top-right corner of Gmail
   - Select "See all settings"

2. **Go to Accounts**
   - Click on the "Accounts" tab

3. **Add Your Email Address**
   - Scroll down to the "Send mail as" section
   - Click "Add another email address"

4. **Enter Your Details**
   - **Name:** [Your Name] (e.g., "Ali Hassan")
   - **Email address:** [Your Email] (e.g., "ali.h@buyersclub.com.au")
   - **Treat as an alias:** ✅ Check this box (recommended)
   - Click "Next Step"

5. **Verify Your Email**
   - Gmail will send a verification email to your address
   - Check your inbox and click the verification link in the email
   - Return to Gmail settings and click "Verify"

6. **Confirm Setup**
   - Once verified, your email will appear in the "Send mail as" list
   - Make sure it's set as the default or available for selection

### Important Notes:
- This allows the system to send emails from your email address
- You must verify the email address by clicking the link Gmail sends
- If you don't receive the verification email, check your spam folder
- This setup only needs to be done once

### Need Help?
If you encounter any issues, please contact [Support Contact/Email]

Thank you,
[Your Name]
